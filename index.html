Org Consolidation Strategies to Improve Customer Experience
Merging 2+ companies has never been easy, and in today’s digital world, it is only getting more complex. Enterprises are faced with migrating, integrating and redesigning sometimes hundreds of on-premises, cloud and SaaS technologies, causing major gaps in a company’s customer experience strategy. In this session, we’ll will into a series of recommendati.ons to ensure your roadmap enables customer-centric capabilities, exploring:
  • The challenge of disconnected and unassociated processes
  • Evaluation criteria for shared or distinct Salesforce instances
  • Tools and considerations for connecting separate Salesforce instances

Selling Your Salesforce Solutions to Stakeholders
Becoming the trusted advisor is the pinnacle of any Salesforce consultant/customer relationship. And while both consultants and stakeholders typically start a relationship aiming for a true partnership, becoming an advisor often proves harder than expected. In this session, we will explore:
  • How to leverage a series of consulting and sales techniques to promote your solutions
  • Tips and tricks of consulting, personality matching and trouble indicators that help you have more productive conversations with stakeholders
